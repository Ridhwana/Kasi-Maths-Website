Our Mission

KasiMaths is a low-cost, scalable, high impact, fun, interactive and tutor driven after-school Maths Enrichment Hub in low-income areas.

KasiMaths leverages on key community partners and is designed to help students from underprivileged township schools to develop mathematical skills, gain confidence in their mathematical abilities, increase their enjoyment of mathematics and in the long-term, help students to pass mathematics at matric level and take up STEM courses at tertiary level.
